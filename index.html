What is it?

Naval Knockout is an iOS game by Ivan Sanchez (@ivanfer) and David Cottrell (@dcottr). Play against your friends in this turn-based cannon blasting ship game. Avoid mines and reefs while navigating your multiple ship types across the sea to sink your enemies with cannons, torpedoes and mines, and destroy your opponent's base before they do!
